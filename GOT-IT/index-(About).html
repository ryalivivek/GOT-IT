GOTIT

- Connecting students with their lost belongings.

Project Overview :

          GOTIT is a web application designed to bridge the gap between students and their lost items. In today's fast-paced environment, personal belongings can easily be misplaced. GOTIT offers a platform where students can report lost items and help others by providing information if they find an item. With secure login and verification, it ensures that only genuine users are involved, fostering trust within the college community.

Features of GOTIT

  • Secure Sign-Up and OTP Verification: Students sign up with roll number, mobile number, and verified ID proof (PDF).
  • Report and Track Lost Items: Raise complaints for lost items and get notifications if others help.
  • Community Collaboration: View complaints from others and help by providing recovery details.
  • Admin Oversight: Admin can monitor activities, manage user access, and ensure valid complaints.

Clientele

GOTIT is specifically designed for college students and administrators to streamline the process of finding lost items and helping others recover theirs.
